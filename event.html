ECO-WALK 2024

Eco-Walk 2024 Join us for Eco-Walk 2024, a city-wide walkathon dedicated to raising awareness about pollution and environmental sustainability. This event is more than just a walk; it's a step towards a cleaner, greener future. Gather your friends, family, and colleagues to walk with us as we traverse the city's most iconic spots, all while advocating for better environmental practices. Along the way, you'll learn about pollution's impact and how you can make a difference. Let's walk together for a cause that matters!

Workshops

Dive deep into environmental topics with our series of engaging workshops! Held monthly, these sessions cover a wide range of subjects from waste reduction and energy efficiency to urban gardening and climate change. Each workshop features expert speakers, practical tips, and interactive activities designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to make a positive impact. Whether you're a seasoned environmentalist or just starting your journey, our workshops offer valuable insights and inspiration.

Green Fair

Discover the future of sustainable living at the Green Fair! This exciting event brings together innovators and vendors showcasing the latest in eco-friendly products and practices. From biodegradable goods to cutting-edge green technologies, the Green Fair is your one-stop destination for all things sustainable. Explore, shop, and learn how you can contribute to a greener planet. Enjoy informative booths, interactive demonstrations, and connect with like-minded individuals passionate about protecting our environment.
